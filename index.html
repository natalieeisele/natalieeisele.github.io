About Me

I began my courses at Indiana University as a marketing student through the business school but felt that something was missing. I have loved reading and writing since I learned to do both, and it did not occur to me until about halfway through my time here that I could have a career doing both. One of my closest friends is a journalism major, and she constantly talked about how much she loved her classes. I wanted to feel that passion for my career too. Now, as a journalism major specializing in public relations and minoring in marketing, I feel as though I have found my niche. I am interning at the Indiana Memorial Union as a social media coordinator and at a PR firm called rock paper scissors, inc. as a music publicity intern this semester. I hope to start my career at a PR firm after graduating in December and plan to freelance for magazines and online publications as well. I have lived in Indiana my entire life, growing up in and around Indianapolis, and I hope to use my education as a platform to travel and live in as many places as possible.
